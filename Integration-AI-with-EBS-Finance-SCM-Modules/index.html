Integration AI with EBS Finance SCM Modules

Author: Kesari Kumar Durbaka
Download PDF Back to all papers

Abstract

This paper presents a practical architecture for adding targeted AI capabilities to Oracle E‑Business Suite Finance and Supply Chain modules without disrupting core processes. It covers decision support, forecasting signals, and knowledge retrieval with clear integration points (FBDI, Open Interface tables, seeded APIs, and event subscriptions), orchestration patterns, and controls for auditability and change management. A test plan and KPIs are included to measure impact on exception rates, processing latency, and month‑end effort.
