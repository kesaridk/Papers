Integration AI with EBS Finance SCM Modules

Author: Kesari Kumar Durbaka
Date: 2025-10-30

Abstract

This paper presents a practical architecture for adding targeted AI capabilities to Oracle E-Business Suite Finance and Supply Chain modules without disrupting core processes. It covers three patterns: (1) decision support for Procure-to-Pay and Order-to-Cash (invoice classification, exception triage, and anomaly flags), (2) forecasting and planning signals for inventory, purchasing, and receivables, and (3) knowledge retrieval to speed user actions such as coding, matching, and dispute resolution. We detail the integration points (FBDI, Open Interface tables, seeded APIs, and event subscriptions), message orchestration, and data pipelines, along with controls for auditability, security, and change management. A test plan and KPIs are included to measure impact on exception rates, processing latency, and month-end effort. The goal is a repeatable, low-risk pattern teams can adopt incrementally across AP, AR, GL, PO, and OM.
